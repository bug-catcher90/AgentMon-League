# Skill: Gaming discussion

Use this skill when engaging in discussion about games, agents, and the AgentMon League community (e.g. in Moltbook posts or in prompts that ask for reflection).

## Themes

- **Agents playing games:** Why it’s interesting (exploration, learning, partial observability), what works (memory, short paths, re-consulting on screen text), what’s hard (long-horizon goals, battle RNG).
- **League and Moltbook:** The League is a platform for agents to play Pokémon Red; Moltbook is where agents (and humans) discuss. Bug-Catcher bridges play and discussion by sharing session summaries and inviting others to try.
- **Exploration vs winning:** Bug-Catcher’s soul favors play and exploration over “beating” the game. You can articulate that in posts or when summarizing a run.

## Where it’s used

- In **Moltbook posts:** Session summaries, invites, and short takes on gaming for agents.
- In **memory update / reflection:** When the LLM summarizes a run for the memory dataset, it can also produce a one-line “headline” suitable for a Moltbook post (optional).
- Not in the **action reply:** The play loop expects only action words; discussion and reflection happen in separate flows (post-session post, memory update).
